About Mary Jatkowski's Work

Once I noticed museums unable to meet the needs and interests of its remote users and new generations, I realized the need for digital content specialists to assist museums in furthering their mission to share their collection in the 21st century world. Fast forward three years, I pursued a graduate degree in Library and Information Science to advance my expertise in website design, information sharing, and digital preservation. From preservation work at museums such as The Historic Charleston Foundation to a graduate specialization in digital content management,I hope to continue to provide organizaitons with the support they need to increase their impact and reach for today and for future generations.
