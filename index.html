About Mary

For the past several years, my work has largely been focused on assisting museums in furthering their mission to share their collection in the 21st century through creating online, interative platforms. From digital preservation work at museums such as Historic Charleston Foundation to a graduate specialization in digital content management, the need for musuems to be digitally accessibile is increasing. With this need on the rise, it is my hope to continue to provide museums and historic organizations with the innovative tools and technical support they need to increase their impact and reach for today and future generations.
